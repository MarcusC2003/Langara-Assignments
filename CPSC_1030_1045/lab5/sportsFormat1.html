My Favorite Sport - VolleyBall

Volleyball is a sport played by 2 teams which usually has 6 players
however different types of volleyball may have varying amounts. The goal of the game
is to not let the ball touch the ground. Each team is allowed 3 touched before they
must volley the ball over a net to the other team. if the ball touches the ground
or it goes out of bounds, the round is over.
